Corbado provides three products to help you track authentication journeys, enhance security and user experience, and deliver passkey-first authentication in your application:

<CardGroup cols={1}>
<Card title="Corbado Observe: Authentication Tracking & Conversion Insights" icon="chart-line" href="/corbado-observe/overview">
  Track authentication journeys across your product so you can understand user behavior, identify drop-offs, and improve conversion. Corbado Observe captures structured events from your application and maps them to a clear data model.
</Card>
<Card title="Corbado Connect: Enterprise Passkey Integration" icon="building" href="/corbado-connect/overview">
  Designed for enterprises with existing Identity Providers (IdPs) or CIAM solutions. Corbado Connect layers passkey functionality on top of your current authentication system, enabling a gradual, risk-free transition to passwordless authentication without migrating users or overhauling your infrastructure.
</Card>
<Card title="Corbado Complete: Passkey-First Identity Platform" icon="laptop-code" href="/corbado-complete/overview">
  A complete, standalone authentication solution for new applications or those looking to replace their entire authentication stack. It's ideal for developers and small to medium-sized businesses who want a quick and easy way to implement secure, passkey-first authentication.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

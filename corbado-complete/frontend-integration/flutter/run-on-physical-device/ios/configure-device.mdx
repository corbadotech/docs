<Steps>
    <Step title="Enable iPhone Developer Mode">
        <div style={{
            overflow: "auto",
            justifyContent: "center",
            alignItems: "center",
            display: "flex",
            gap: "20px",
            flexWrap: "wrap"
        }}>
            <div style={{width: 200}}>
                ![Deverloper Mode](/images/frontend-integration/flutter/iOS-device-configuration-1.webp)
            </div>
            <div style={{ flex: 1 }}>
                To allow Flutter to install your app on your device, you must enable **Developer Mode**:

                1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
                2. Go to **Privacy & Security**
                3. Scroll down and tap **Developer Mode**
                4. Toggle it **on**

                <Note>If you have previously run apps from Xcode on your device, Developer Mode is likely already enabled.</Note>
            </div>
        </div>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enable Associated Domains Development">
        <div style={{
            overflow: "auto",
            marginBottom: "20px",
            justifyContent: "center",
            alignItems: "center",
            display: "flex",
            gap: "20px",
            flexWrap: "wrap"
        }}>
            <div style={{width: 200}}>
                ![Associated Domains Development](/images/frontend-integration/flutter/iOS-device-configuration-3.webp)
            </div>
            <div style={{ flex: 1 }}>
                To enable your device to bypass the Apple-App-Site-Association (AASA) file cache using `?mode=developer`, you
                must enable **Associated Domains Development**:

                1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
                2. Scroll down to **Developer**
                3. Tap **Associated Domains Development**
                4. Toggle it **on**
            </div>
        </div>
    </Step>
</Steps>
